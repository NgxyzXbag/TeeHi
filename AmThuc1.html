Quintessence of central cuisine

With a long and narrow terrain, the Central region is a land subject to many natural disasters, floods, and harsh climates, so the people here are also more industrious, the food also focuses on going into depth. More on the taste, not too fussy or ostentatious, this is also the only common feature that creates the flavor of Central cuisine.

Quintessence of southern cuisine

As sincere and sweet as the Southern people, their dishes are equally affected. When it comes to Southern cuisine, many people will immediately think of the saying: "There are fish in the river, vegetables on the shore". The sentence almost encapsulates all the inherent characteristics of Southern cuisine.
